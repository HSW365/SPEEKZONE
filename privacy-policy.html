Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

SpeekZone ("we", "our", "us"), operated by HOODSTAR ENT LLC / HSW365Media, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, password (hashed)
  • Profile data: bio, avatar, website links you provide
  • Content you create: podcast episodes, show descriptions, cover art
  • Usage data: episode plays, listen duration, app interactions
  • Device information: device type, OS version, app version
  • Payment data: handled entirely by Apple In-App Purchase — we never see your card details

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve SpeekZone
  • To distribute your podcast to partner platforms
  • To send you notifications about your account and shows
  • To display analytics about your podcast's performance
  • To prevent fraud and maintain platform security

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Distribution partners (Spotify, Apple Podcasts, etc.) — only your show's public metadata and audio files
  • Cloud infrastructure providers (servers, storage) under confidentiality agreements
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law

Apple In-App Purchases

All subscription payments are processed by Apple. We receive confirmation of successful purchases but never your payment card details. Manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID Account Settings.

Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by emailing hsw365media@gmail.com. Deleted accounts are purged within 30 days.

Your Rights

  • Access or export your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Children

SpeekZone is rated 17+ and not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
