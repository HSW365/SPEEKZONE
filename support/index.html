Common questions

How do I delete my account?
Open Profile → tap the menu icon (top right) → Delete Account. This is immediate and permanent.

How do I cancel my Verified subscription?
Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions → SpeekZone → Cancel.

How do I report a room or a user?
Email us at the address above with details and we'll investigate promptly.
